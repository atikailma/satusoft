    About Us

    We are Odoo Partner. In addition to 30 main applications, Odoo has more than 16000 apps to cover a wide variety of business needs. It is used by over 3.700.000 users worldwide ranging from startup companies (1 user) to large enterprises (300,000+ users).

    We are a software solution company that offers single solution for all your business needs in the form of Odoo Enterprise Resource Planning. ERP software includes CRM, HRD, POS, e-commerce, inventory, accounting, manufacturing, and many more.

    Our services

    ODOO ENTERPRISE

    We offer consultation and implementation of Odoo Enterprise. We will support in the migration and data management from old system.

    ODOO CLOUD HOSTING

    With cloud hosting, you can access Odoo from anywhere in the world concurrently. This is especially important for businesses with plenty of locations.

    ODOO MOBILE

    The mobile version of Odoo is available in the form of native Android and ios apps, and allows extra flexibility and portability for your employees.

    ODOO COMMUNITY

    We can also help to implement the community version of Odoo which is open source.

    ODOO ON SITE

    With on site hosting, it is more suitable for small businesses or businesses with stringent security requirements.

    ODOO COSTUMIZATION

    We customizes Odoo according to your company needs since each country usually has specific requirements in term of tax, insurance, etc.
